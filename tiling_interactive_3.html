Why hexagons win: The interior angle of a regular hexagon is exactly 120°. Three hexagons meet at each vertex: 3 × 120° = 360°. Perfect fit — no gap, no overlap. No other regular polygon with more sides achieves this.
🐝 This is what the bees chose.
The hexagon is the unique solution to the Honeycomb Conjecture — proven in 1999 after 2,000 years of mathematics. Nature found it first.
